# 1. Background

Based on keyword research data (US market):

| Keyword              | Monthly Volume | Difficulty (SEMrush) |
| -------------------- | -------------- | -------------------- |
| soundcloud to mp3    | 90,500         | 20                   |
| soundcloud to wav    | 5,400          | 15                   |
| soundcloud converter | 3,600          | 19                   |

Current Situation:

* We already have:

  * SoundCloud to WAV
  * SoundCloud Playlist Downloader
* We DO NOT have:

  * Dedicated SoundCloud to MP3 landing page
  * Format cluster pages (FLAC, AAC, etc.)
  * Audio Download Hub structure
  * FAQ schema optimization

Problem:

We are currently targeting a **low-volume sub-keyword (WAV)** instead of the main traffic keyword (MP3).

Opportunity:

Build a full **SoundCloud Download Ecosystem** to capture 200k+ monthly search demand.

Add structured data for:

* Is SoundCloud to MP3 legal?
* How to download SoundCloud as MP3?
* Can I download private tracks?
* What is the best SoundCloud converter?
* Is this free?

## 4.2 Internal Linking Strategy

Each SoundCloud-related page must:

* Link to all other SoundCloud tools
* Link back to Audio Hub
* Use keyword-rich anchor text

Example anchors:

* Convert SoundCloud to MP3
* Download SoundCloud playlist
* High-quality SoundCloud WAV
