## 1. Background & Opportunity

Millions of users search every month for ways to test their printers before or after setup. Common problems include incorrect colors, missing ink lines, alignment issues, and grayscale inaccuracies.

Existing solutions such as printatestpage.com provide basic printable pages but lack customization, brand-specific guidance, and modern user experience.

This product aims to become a **comprehensive printer test toolkit**, combining instant printable test pages with SEO-optimized content to capture high-intent organic traffic.
