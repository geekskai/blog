## 3. User Experience & Flow

### Quiz Paths

**A. Modern Etiquette Mode**
20 questions based on real-life behavior (courtesy, respect, integrity etc.)

**B. Knight’s Code Mode** _(optional toggle)_
15 questions inspired by traditional knight virtues (honor, courage, loyalty, humility, courtesy).

**User can switch modes mid-survey for hybrid scoring.**

---

## 4. Question Types

1. **Likert Scale (Strongly Disagree → Strongly Agree)**
2. **Scenario Choice**
   e.g. _“You see someone struggling with heavy doors. You…”_
3. **Mini Narrative Judgement**
   e.g. _“While jousting in a tournament, you notice your opponent injured…”_
4. **Time-pressure situational choice**
   _Enhance engagement and emotional processing._

## 5. Scoring & Results

### Scoring Model

- Weighted scoring per section (0–100 total)
- Separate “Modern Score” & “Knight Score”
- Optional mixed score breakdown

### Result Sections

**A. Persona Title**

- Modern categories:
  • The Kind Observer
  • Everyday Knight
  • True Gentleman
  • Chivalry Champion
- Historical categories:
  • Squire in Training
  • Knight of Honor
  • Paladin of Virtues
  • Champion of the Round

**B. Divider Charts**

- Radar chart of core traits: Courtesy, Respect, Integrity, Courage, Loyalty

**C. Personalized Tips**

- Immediate next action habit
- Daily etiquette micro-challenge

**D. Share Copy + Visual Card**

- Auto-generated share blurb + image
  e.g.
  > “I scored 82 as an Everyday Knight! How chivalrous are YOU?”
